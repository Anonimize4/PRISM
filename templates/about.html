About PRISM

Revolutionizing internship management through innovative technology and seamless collaboration.

Our Mission

PRISM (Professional Internship and Recruitment Information System Management) is dedicated to bridging the gap between educational institutions, students, and industry partners. We believe that every student deserves access to meaningful internship opportunities that align with their career goals and academic pursuits.

Our platform empowers universities to efficiently manage their internship programs, enables companies to discover and nurture top talent, and provides students with the tools they need to succeed in their professional journeys.

Our Story

How PRISM came to be

2020: The Beginning

Founded by a team of educators and industry professionals who recognized the inefficiencies in traditional internship management processes.

2021: First Release

Launched our initial platform with core features for application management and basic reporting.

2022: Expansion

Added advanced analytics, multi-role support, and mobile responsiveness to serve a growing user base.

2023: Innovation

Introduced AI-powered matching algorithms and real-time collaboration tools to enhance user experience.

2024: Global Reach

Expanded to serve institutions worldwide, with support for multiple languages and international standards.
